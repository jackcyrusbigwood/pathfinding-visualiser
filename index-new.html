To create a maze
(1) place walls by clicking the black button and then clicking the tiles of the grid,
(2) place the start location by clicking the green button and then clicking a tile of the grid, and
(3) place the end location by clicking the blue button and then clicking a tile of the grid.

To unplace a wall/start/end tile, re-click it when the corresponding button is active.
To override an existing wall/start/end tile, click it when a different corresponding button is active.

When finished creating a maze, run the BFS pathfinding algorithm by clicking the purple button.
